#### 💻 &nbsp; About Me

Hello! I'm a front-end engineer based in Taiwan. Currently, I work at Cathay United Bank in the DDT Team (數數發), where I focus on building and maintaining features for the online banking platform (Cube MyBank).
<br />
My role involves crafting responsive and accessible web interfaces that serve millions of users, balancing usability, security, and performance. I work closely with designers, backend engineers, and product managers to deliver seamless banking experiences.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about thoughtful UI/UX, clean code, and improving developer experience. I enjoy exploring frontend technologies like React, TypeScript, and Next.js, and I’m always curious about better ways to build scalable and maintainable applications.
